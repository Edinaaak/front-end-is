SEARCH FUNCTIONALITY


You don't have to worry about which departures operate according to the summer timetable, which ones on holidays, and which ones on weekends. Simply select the date and approximate travel time, and "Polazak" will display the valid timetable for that day for all bus and train lines. If you only choose the name of the settlement during the search, the results will include all stations in the selected settlement.

SEARCH WITH TRANSFERS


The team behind the "Polazak" website puts a lot of effort into connecting existing lines of different transport providers if there are no direct departures on the requested route. Users of the website are presented with options for transfers. The search with transfers lists all those departures that, travel time, can take the passenger to the desired destination with one transfer at one of the intermediate stations.
